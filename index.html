LOVE OF JESUS

The love of Jesus is boundless, unconditional, and life-transforming. It is a love that embraces the broken, forgives the sinner, and restores the weary. Through His sacrifice on the cross, Jesus demonstrated the greatest act of love—giving His life so that we may be saved and reconciled with God. His love is not based on our worthiness but on His grace, offering hope, peace, and eternal life to all who believe in Him. No matter our past or struggles, His love remains steadfast, guiding us with mercy and filling our hearts with joy and purpose The love of Jesus is the purest expression of God’s heart toward humanity. It is a love that never fails, never gives up, and never turns away. Jesus showed His love through compassion, healing the sick, comforting the brokenhearted, and welcoming the outcasts. His love reaches beyond our failures and weaknesses, offering forgiveness


HELL FIRE

Jesus spoke about hellfire as a place of judgment for those who reject God and choose a life of sin. He described it as a place of "outer darkness" where there is "weeping and gnashing of teeth" (Matthew 25:30). In Mark 9:43-48, He warned that it is better to cut off a sinful hand or foot than to be cast into "hell, where the fire never goes out." He also referred to Gehenna, a term for hell, as a place of unquenchable fire (Matthew 10:28), emphasizing the eternal nature of separation from God. However, Jesus' warnings about hell were not meant to condemn but to lead people to repentance and salvation. His ultimate message was one of love and redemption, urging all to turn to Him for forgiveness and eternal life.
